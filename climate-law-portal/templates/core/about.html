About this portal

The {{ SITE_NAME }} is a free, open research tool that indexes climate & environmental case law from Pakistan's courts and environmental tribunals. It is inspired by Pakistan Law Site and aims to make environmental jurisprudence searchable for researchers, students, lawyers, and journalists.

What's inside

  • 5,000+ environmental tribunal complaints and judgments.
  • Full-text keyword search across title, summary, and judgment.
  • Filtering by year and violation type.
  • Stable, citable URLs for each case.

Stack

  • Backend: Django + SQLite
  • Frontend: Server-rendered Django templates & minimal CSS
  • Hosting: Render free tier

This portal is provided for research and educational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice.
